This writeup summarizes ongoing efforts to tune the **optical absorption of ZnTe cores** while maintaining viable **ZnMnS shell growth**.

The project is framed around a chemistry-first question: how do **precursor identity**, **solvent environment**, and **growth conditions** jointly determine whether the system reaches the desired optical and structural regime?
