Alongside experimental work, I use **molecular modeling** and **theoretical chemistry** to understand how ligands, solvents, and local coordination environments influence nanoscale materials behavior.

This direction includes modeling chemically complex systems, analyzing interactions at nanomaterial interfaces, and connecting computational insight to experimentally observed structure-property relationships.

The long-term goal is to use computation not just for explanation, but for **design guidance** in future synthesis campaigns.
